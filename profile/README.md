## Who We Are

Dextera Consulting is a technology and innovation consulting firm specialising in the design and delivery of large-scale digital projects. Harnessing **Artificial Intelligence**, **IoT**, and **Smart Data Analytics**, we work with governments, healthcare organisations, and enterprises to drive meaningful transformation.

Nearly two decades of experience. Strategic partnerships with leading global organisations. A commitment to solutions that create real value for society, businesses, and people.

We are certified under **ISO 9001:2015** (Quality Management) and **ISO 27001:2023** (Information Security) — ensuring reliability and trust in every engagement.

<br/>

## What We Do

### 🏥 E-Health & Telemedicine
Design, implementation, and management of e-health and telemedicine projects — including digital health platforms, patient-centric systems, and smart care technologies. Our flagship products include **Dextera Telemedicine Solutions**, **DEXHealthHub**, and **UpToDate** clinical decision support.

### 🌐 Digital Transformation & IoT
End-to-end digital transformation projects, IoT-driven applications, and Smart City solutions — from smart parking and environmental monitoring to fully integrated connected ecosystems.

### 📊 Business Intelligence & Data Analytics
Tailored BI and analytics solutions that help organisations make faster, smarter, data-driven decisions.
